How to Play

Three tournaments, three histories. Pick your competition: UCL Legends (Champions League 1956–2024 · 66 squads · 29 clubs), Copa Libertadores (South America 1960–2024 · 31 squads), World Cup Legends (World Cup 1930–2022 · 65 national teams). Each tournament has its own eras, opponents and player pool.

Choose a format. Old Cup: pure knockout from round one, no group stage. Classic: 3 group matches then playoffs. New Wave (UCL only): 6 league-phase matches with an 8-point qualification threshold, then playoffs. Copa Libertadores and World Cup support Old Cup and Classic only.

Pick an era and difficulty. Each tournament has its own eras (e.g. UCL: 1960s, 1980s… or All Time for the full pool). Easy = 5 rerolls, Normal = 3, Hard = 2. Difficulty also scales opponent strength. On Normal and Hard, reroll is locked for the last 3 slots — save your moves when it matters.

Pick a formation and draft your XI. Ten formations available: 4-3-3, 4-4-2, 4-2-3-1, 3-5-2, 4-1-4-1, 3-4-3, 5-3-2, 4-5-1, 5-4-1, 3-6-1. All 3 draft cards always belong to the same rating tier: ★★★★ Elite (r=10), ★★★ High (r=9), ★★ Mid (r=8), or ★ Base (r≤7). Use ↺ Reroll to change tier and options — each reroll can flip the pick quality. ★ players (9.5+) carry an xG bonus. UCL and Copa show club names; World Cup shows nation and year (e.g. 🇧🇷 Brazil · 1970).

Momentum. Winning builds momentum (up to +3), losing erodes it (down to −3). Each point shifts your xG by ±0.05 — a winning run makes you harder to stop.

Tactic & scout report. Before each match a scout report reveals the opponent's likely style — High Press, Possession, or Low Block. Countering correctly gives an xG advantage. Momentum rewards winning runs: each consecutive win adds +0.05 xG, each loss −0.05 (range ±3).

Tournament structure. Old Cup: straight knockout from the start (R16 → QF → SF → Final). Classic: 3 group matches (top 2 advance) then playoffs. New Wave (UCL): 6 league matches — earn ≥ 8 points to qualify, then playoffs. One loss in the knockout phase ends the campaign. Draws go to penalties.

Manager. After completing your XI, choose a manager from 3 candidates. Each has a preferred formation — perfect match +8% xG, two lines matching +5%, one +2%, none +0%. Final choice, no rerolls.

Stars. Win by 2+ goals = ⭐⭐⭐  ·  Win by 1 = ⭐⭐  ·  Draw or pens = ⭐  ·  Loss = 0. Stars accumulate in the Hall of Fame alongside your full campaign record.

Cos'è Decempionz?

Decempionz è un progetto fan non ufficiale e gratuito, nato dalla passione per la storia del calcio internazionale. Celebra decenni di Champions League, Copa Libertadores e Mondiali attraverso un gioco di draft strategico. Non è affiliato, sponsorizzato né approvato da UEFA, FIFA, CONMEBOL o da alcun club calcistico.

Uso dei nomi

I nomi di calciatori, allenatori e squadre sono utilizzati esclusivamente a scopo di intrattenimento culturale e storico, senza fini commerciali. Nessun dato ufficiale protetto da copyright viene riprodotto.
