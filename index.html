How to Play

Choose your era. Pick from 10 historic eras (1961–2023) or go All Time for the complete 58-squad pool spanning 27 legendary clubs. Each era comes with its own tournament teams and draft pool.

Set difficulty. Easy (10 skips), Normal (7), Hard (5). Difficulty also adjusts opponent strength — Hard opponents hit harder, Easy ones are slightly weakened.

Pick a formation. Ten options: 4-3-3, 4-4-2, 4-2-3-1, 3-5-2, 4-1-4-1, 3-4-3, 5-3-2, 4-5-1, 5-4-1, 3-6-1. Each slot has a fixed position requirement — plan ahead before drafting.

Blind draft. Players are revealed one at a time. Each card shows the club colors, rating, and position. Accept to add them to your XI, or Pass to skip. Star players rated 8.5+ carry an individual attack bonus — prioritise them. Players that don't fit any open slot are auto-skipped.

Tactic & scout report. Before each match a scout report reveals the opponent's likely style — High Press, Possession, or Low Block. Countering correctly gives a significant xG advantage: Press beats Low Block, Possession beats Press, Low Block beats Possession.

Momentum. Winning builds momentum (up to +3), losing erodes it (down to −3). Each point shifts your xG by ±0.05 — a winning run makes you harder to stop.

Group stage. 3 matches. Win = 3 pts, Draw = 1, Loss = 0. Finish in the top 2 to advance. Bottom two are eliminated.

Knockouts. Round of 16 → Quarter-Final → Semi-Final → Final. One loss ends the campaign. Draws go to penalties.

Stars. Win by 2+ goals = ⭐⭐⭐  ·  Win by 1 = ⭐⭐  ·  Draw or pens = ⭐  ·  Loss = 0.
