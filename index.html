How to Play

Choose your era. Pick from 10 historic eras (1961–2023) or go All Time for the complete 61-squad pool spanning 28 legendary clubs. Each era sets the tournament opponents and the draft pool you'll build your XI from.

Set difficulty. Easy (5 rerolls), Normal (3), Hard (2). Difficulty also adjusts opponent strength — Hard opponents hit harder, Easy ones are slightly weakened.

Pick a formation. Ten options: 4-3-3, 4-4-2, 4-2-3-1, 3-5-2, 4-1-4-1, 3-4-3, 5-3-2, 4-5-1, 5-4-1, 3-6-1. Each slot has a fixed position requirement — choose wisely before the draft begins.

Draft your XI. Three cards are dealt at once. Tap the one you want — it fills the best available slot automatically. Not happy with the options? Hit ↺ Reroll to get three new cards, but rerolls are scarce. Legendary players (★ rated 9.5+) carry a bonus to your attack. Cards that don't fit any open slot are skipped automatically.

Tactic & scout report. Before each match a scout report reveals the opponent's likely style — High Press, Possession, or Low Block. Countering correctly gives a significant xG advantage: Press beats Low Block, Possession beats Press, Low Block beats Possession.

Momentum. Winning builds momentum (up to +3), losing erodes it (down to −3). Each point shifts your xG by ±0.05 — a winning run makes you harder to stop.

Group stage. 3 matches. Win = 3 pts, Draw = 1, Loss = 0. Finish in the top 2 to advance. Bottom two are eliminated.

Knockouts. Round of 16 → Quarter-Final → Semi-Final → Final. One loss ends the campaign. Draws go to penalties.

Stars. Win by 2+ goals = ⭐⭐⭐  ·  Win by 1 = ⭐⭐  ·  Draw or pens = ⭐  ·  Loss = 0.
